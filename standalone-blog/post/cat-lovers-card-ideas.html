10 Purr-fect Card Ideas for Cat Lovers

EGreet Team August 8, 2024 5 min read

For the cat lover in your life, nothing beats a card that celebrates their feline friends. Whether it's for a birthday, a thank you, or just because, these creative cat card ideas are sure to make them smile (and maybe even purr with delight!).

1. Paw Print Pattern

Use a paw print stamp or draw your own to create a cute background pattern. It's simple, effective, and instantly recognizable to any pet owner.

What You'll Need:

  • White or kraft cardstock
  • Black ink pad or marker
  • Cat stickers (optional)

2. "You're the Cat's Meow"

Combine a vintage cat illustration with the classic phrase "You're the cat's meow." It's a sweet compliment wrapped in a stylish design.

3. Yarn Ball Surprise

Glue a small tangle of real yarn onto the card, perhaps "unraveling" to spell out a name or a word like "Love." It adds a fun, tactile element.

4. Cat Silhouette at Sunset

Paint a warm sunset background (orange, pink, purple) and glue a black silhouette of a cat sitting on a fence or looking out a window. It's artistic and peaceful.

5. "Have a Purr-fect Day"

Who can resist a good pun? Draw a happy cat face and write "Have a purr-fect day!" It's a great go-to for birthdays or general well-wishes.

6. Peek-a-Boo Cat

Design the card so that a cat's face is peeking over the bottom edge or from the side. It gives the impression of a curious kitty checking in on them.

7. Grumpy Cat Humor

Channel the internet's favorite feline with a card featuring a grumpy-looking cat and a funny, sarcastic caption like "I got you a card. You're welcome."

8. Digital Meowing Card

Send a digital card via EGreet that actually meows when opened! You can choose from cute kitten mews to funny cat sounds for a memorable surprise.

Pro Tip

If you know their cat's name or what they look like, try to customize the card to match. A card featuring a tuxedo cat for a tuxedo cat owner shows extra thoughtfulness.

9. Cat Ear Shape

Cut the top of the card to resemble cat ears. Draw a simple face below the ears for a minimalist and modern cat card.

10. "Feline Good"

Another great pun! A card with a relaxed, sleeping cat and the text "Feeling Good" (or "Feline Good") is perfect for a get-well-soon or relaxation theme.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are some good cat puns for cards?
"You're pawsome," "Happy Purr-thday," "Best fishes," "Meow or never," and "Stay pawsitive" are all crowd-pleasers.
Can I put my own cat on a card?
Yes! With EGreet's custom card maker, you can upload a photo of your own pet to create a truly one-of-a-kind greeting.
Are these cards only for birthdays?
Not at all. Cat cards work for thank yous, congratulations, get well soon, or just to make a friend laugh.
